Dancer, Actor, and Singer

Her Rise and Fall...

  • Aaliyah was born on January 16th, 1979 in Brooklyn New York.
  • While living in Michigan, 10 year old Aaliyah appeared on the show Star Search where she performed alongside Gladys Knight.
  • At age 12 Aaliyah signed on with Jive Records, Blackground Records.
  • She was introduced to R. Kelly who became her mentor as well as the lead songwriter and producer of her debut album, Age Aint Nothing But a Number.
  • Her album sold 3 million copies in the United States and was certified double platinum by the Recording Industry Association of America.
  • After her illegal marriage with singer R. Kelly, she ended her contract with Jive Records and signed on with Atlantic Records.
  • Aaliyah worked with Timbaland and Missy Elliot on second album One in a Million, which sold over 8 million copies worldwide.
  • In 2000, Aaliyah began her acting career with her first movie Romeo Must Die. Her single Try Again is a product of her contribution to the films soundtrack.
  • Try Again earned Aaliyah a Grammy Award Nomination for Best Female R&B Vocalist.
  • After completing Romeo Must Die, Aaliyah filmed her role in Queen of the Damned, and released her final album Aaliyah in 2001.
  • On August 25,2001, Aaliyah and eight others were killed in a plane crash in the Bahamas after filming the music video for Rock the Boat.
  • The pilot of the plane, Luis Morales III, was unlicensed at the time of the accident and toxicology tests showed he had traces of cocaine in his system.
  • Aaliyah has sold an estimated 24 to 32 million albums worldwide. She is also listed by Billboard as the tenth most successful female R&B artist of the past 25 years, and the 27th most successful R&B artist in history.
